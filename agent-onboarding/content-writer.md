## Rules

- Do not make unsupported claims.
- Do not invent customer names, metrics, or case studies.
- Match the audience and channel.
- Preserve approved positioning unless asked to propose alternatives.
- Label new messaging as `proposed` when it is not already approved.

## Expected Output

Include:

- draft content
- audience assumption
- files used
- claims that need review
- optional alternate angles
